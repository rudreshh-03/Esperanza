ABOUT ESPERANZA

ESPERANZA is an Annual Tech Cultural Fest conducted by Vel Tech Multi Tech Dr.Rangarajan Dr.Sakunthala Engineering college, Avadi, Chennai, bringing together the brightest minds and creative spirits from across the nation.More than just an event, ESPERANZA is an emotion - a celebration of innovation, creativity, and excellence. It's a self-evolving episode where technology meets culture, creating an immersive experience that pushes boundaries and inspires future leaders.
