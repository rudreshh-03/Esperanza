ABOUT ESPERANZA

ESPERANZA is an Annual Tech Cultural Fest conducted by Vel Tech Multi Tech Dr.Rangarajan Dr.Sakunthala Engineering college, Avadi, Chennai, bringing together the brightest minds and creative spirits from across the nation.More than just an event, ESPERANZA is an emotion - a celebration of innovation, creativity, and excellence. It's a self-evolving episode where technology meets culture, creating an immersive experience that pushes boundaries and inspires future leaders.

Photography - Lens Legacy
You see the world differently. While the rest of us are busy taking blurry photos of our food for Snapchat, you’re out there crafting art. Whether it’s a breathtaking scenery, or just an aesthetically pleasing frame of a cup, Lens Legacy is your moment to prove that your photography isn’t just ‘good’—it’s: LEGEN-wait for it- DARY.

Short Film - Frame by Frame
Hollywood called. They said they might need you... but first, prove yourself here. Everyone’s got a story, but can you tell yours in a way that keeps us from scrolling away? Frame by Frame is where storytellers, visionaries, and those who believe they’re the next Scorsese get to shine. This is where scripts meet screens, where vision meets execution, and let the frames do the talking.
